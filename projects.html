Project #1

A redesign Instagram collateral for Cal Poly Intertidal Marketing Team

The Blind Date with a Book flyer redesign focused on enhancing readability and engagement while maintaining a festive, romantic aesthetic. The original flyer effectively conveyed the theme but lacked key event details, such as a strong call to action, event location, and mention of giveaways. The new design introduced clearer textual hierarchy, a lighter color scheme, and playful imagery of books and donuts to attract attention. By balancing eye-catching visuals with organized information, the flyer effectively communicated event details in a visually appealing manner.
